One Piece

One piece is a Japanese anime television series produced by Toei Animation that premiered on Fuji TV in October 1999. It is based on Eiichiro Oda's manga series of the same name. The story follows the adventures of Monkey D. Luffy, a boy whose body gained the properties of rubber after unintentionally eating a Devil Fruit. With his crew of pirates, named the Straw Hat Pirates, Luffy explores the Grand Line in search of the world's ultimate treasure known as "One Piece" in order to become the next Pirate King.



Some information about one piece characters are:

The Straw Hats

Monkey.D.Luffy

 Monkey D. Luffy also known as Straw Hat Luffy, is the protagonist of the One Piece franchise. He is the captain of the Straw Hat Pirates and a Yonko that wants to find the One Piece treasure and become the King of the Pirates. He is also the son of Monkey D. Dragon, the grandson of Monkey D. Garp, the adoptive younger brother of Portgas D. Ace and Sabo, and the love interest of the ex-Shichibukai Boa Hancock.

Roronoa Zoro

 Roronoa Zoro is a swordsman and the combatant of the Straw Hat Pirates. Also known as "Pirate Hunter Zoro", he is a pirate, former bounty hunter from the anime and manga series One Piece. He is one of the most versatile fighters in the series and one of the strongest four fighters in the Straw Hat Pirates beside Monkey D. Luffy, Sanji and Jinbe and considered one of the biggest threats by the World Government in the crew after his captain and Jinbei. He is also the most loyal crewmate to Luffy amongst the Straw Hats.

Sanji

 Sanji, born as Vinsmoke Sanji and also known as "Black Leg Sanji", is a pirate and the chef of the Straw Hat Pirates. He is the fifth member of the crew and the fourth to join.

Nami

 Nami is one of the main characters in the anime and manga One Piece. She and Nico Robin are the only female members of the Straw Hats so far. She is also known and considered as the main heroine of the series.
